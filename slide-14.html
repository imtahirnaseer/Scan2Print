Retention Policy (Redis)

Strict TTL enforcement. Any file not printed within 15 minutes is automatically expunged from memory. No recovery possible.

Indexing Strategy (Firestore)

Compound indexes on shop_id + timestamp for fast analytics dashboards. No PII (Personally Identifiable Information) stored in permanent logs.
